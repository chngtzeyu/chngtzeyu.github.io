Data science leverages the growth of technology to solve complex macroeconomic problems and deliver cutting-edge business insights. Join us as we harness the power of statistics and high-performance computing to demonstrate how data science is driving innovation and growth.

Membership Events

LSESU Data Science Society (LSESUDS) is an initiative to bring and teach data science to LSE and for everyone. We welcome both LSE undergraduates and postgraduates who are interested in data science, data mining, statistical modelling, big data analytics, machine learning, and the art of data visualization to turn it into a valuable asset for your academic pursuits and future careers.
chat

Talks & Seminars

Topics include Introduction to Data Science & Big Data Analytics, Internet of Things (IoT), Internet of Everything, Financial Technology (FinTech), Data Science Use Cases in Banking & Finance, Insurance, Health, Politics & Government, Economics, Data Visualization and Business Intelligence, and Big Data Technologies: Hadoop, Sparks Analytics.

class

Coding Classes

Experience an enjoyable and interactive platform that illustrates the real-life applications of data analysis. We will cover basics of R, Python, SAS, SPSS, Hadoop, Sparks, Machine Learning, and Big Data & IT Trend. We will later discuss some machine learning techniques to use in business analytics in various fields: banking, finance, insurance, and health etc.

people

Networking Opportunities

We aim to bring together LSE students and prepare them for careers in data analytics through hands-on practical workshops, public events, and distinguished guests. We never require any prior programming experience--we love curiosity! And since data science is a multidisciplinary field, the society will underline why data science matters and introduce big data analytics from scratch.
